Our Mission

The spiritual journey of Gurudev Shri Dr. Suraj Pratap Ji and his promotion of the path of “Maa Bhagwati Parashakti” has been profound and enduring. Gurudev’s life goal became clear: to propagate “Maa Parashakti Bhagwati” and spread this great path to the masses.

Through his YouTube channel, "Dharam Rahasya," and the "Maa Bhagwati Parashakti Trust," Gurudev has endeavored to achieve this mission. To expand the reach of “Maa Bhagwati Parashakti,” Gurudev has devised plans to ensure its presence is not limited to India but extends globally.

Gurudev focused on developing new resources related to “Maa Bhagwati Parashakti” in multiple languages, including English, Gujarati, Bengali, Tamil, and others. Books, e-books, and audio-video materials were produced to ensure the teachings and practices reach people worldwide.

His goal includes establishing temples and ashrams dedicated to “Maa Bhagwati Parashakti” across India. These centers will serve as hubs for worship, spiritual practices, and dissemination of teachings.

To embrace the digital age, Gurudev prioritized expanding the reach of the "Dharam Rahasya" YouTube channel by introducing multilingual content. Additionally, plans were made to develop a website, mobile app, and social media platforms for disseminating material about “Maa Bhagwati Parashakti.”

For future generations, Gurudev ensured the preservation of the heritage of “Maa Bhagwati Parashakti” through educational, training, and research programs under the trust. His dream is to spread the knowledge and practices of this spiritual path globally, bringing transformation to people's lives and helping them achieve the four objectives of human life.

About Guruji

Gurudev Shri Suraj Pratap Ji was born on October 1, 1989, and spent his childhood in Uttarakhand. From an early age, he displayed devotion and dedication towards the divine forms of Maa Bhagwati. His childhood was surrounded by spiritual energy, influenced heavily by his father, who was also a devout worshipper of the Goddess.

Guruji often visited temples, participated in rituals, and engaged in spiritual practices, supported by his family. A pivotal moment in his life was meeting his spiritual mentor, Bhagat Ji, during his early teenage years. This encounter marked the beginning of his profound spiritual journey.

About Trust (MPDRST)

The Maa Bhagwati Parashakti Dharma Rahasya Seva Trust was established in 2023 by Gurudev Shri Dr. Suraj Pratap Ji to propagate the divine knowledge of Maa Parashakti. The trust focuses on spreading spiritual wisdom, promoting Sanatan Dharma, and engaging in social welfare activities.

MPDRST conducts various initiatives, including environmental conservation, educational programs, and health camps for the underprivileged. Its mission is to establish temples and ashrams where devotees can practice the teachings of Maa Parashakti and embrace spiritual and social development.
