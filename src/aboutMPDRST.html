Inspiration Behind the Trust

The establishment of "Maa Parashakti Dharma Rahasya Seva Trust" (MPDRST) marked a significant milestone in the journey to propagate the divine knowledge and spiritual practices of Maa Bhagwati Parashakti, inspired by the unwavering resolve and vision of Gurudev Shri Dr. Suraj Pratap Ji. Founded in 2023, the Trust aims to disseminate Maa Parashakti's teachings while contributing to societal welfare.

Mission and Vision

The primary mission of MPDRST is to:

  • Spread the divine knowledge and mantras of Maa Parashakti to every individual.
  • Promote spiritual enlightenment and societal welfare through initiatives in education, healthcare, environmental conservation, and community service.
  • Establish a global presence for Maa Parashakti's teachings.

Gurudev Shri Dr. Suraj Pratap Ji envisioned MPDRST as a platform to unify spiritual and social causes, creating a harmonious model that benefits both individuals and society.

Key Activities and Achievements

Spiritual Awareness:

  • Disseminating knowledge about Maa Parashakti through sermons and divine mantras.
  • Conducting discussions and awareness campaigns on Sanatan Dharma through platforms like YouTube ("Dharma Rahasya" channel).
  • Revealing the mystical aspects of Sanatan Dharma and their relevance to modern life.

Community Service:

  • Organizing food donation drives, water donation camps, and distributing spiritual literature.
  • Supporting the underprivileged by providing essential resources and assistance.

Educational Initiatives:

  • Expanding facilities in schools and educational centers, introducing students to Maa Parashakti’s teachings.
  • Conducting workshops and seminars to deepen understanding of spirituality.

Healthcare Efforts:

  • Hosting free health camps for the marginalized and providing necessary medical aid.

Environmental Conservation:

  • Launching tree plantation drives and cleaning rivers and other natural resources.
  • Promoting ecological balance through sustainable practices.

Future Goals and Spiritual Aspirations

Gurudev Shri Dr. Suraj Pratap Ji’s spiritual journey has shaped MPDRST’s vision for the future. Key aspirations include:

  • International Expansion: Translating Maa Parashakti’s teachings into multiple languages, including English, Gujarati, Bengali, and Tamil, and making them accessible worldwide through books, e-books, and digital content.
  • Digital Outreach: Enhancing the reach of the “Dharma Rahasya” YouTube channel and expanding content across websites, mobile apps, and social media platforms.
  • Temple and Ashram Construction: Establishing grand temples and ashrams dedicated to Maa Parashakti, enabling devotees to experience divine darshan and wisdom.
  • Preserving Spiritual Heritage: Developing educational and research programs to ensure the legacy of Maa Parashakti’s teachings for future generations.

Social Impact

Through its diverse initiatives, MPDRST has become a beacon of hope for many:

  • Encouraging individuals to align their lives with Maa Parashakti’s path, fostering spiritual growth and societal harmony.
  • Supporting devotees and society at large with selfless service and unwavering dedication.

Join Us

MPDRST invites everyone to be a part of this divine journey. Whether through participation in its initiatives, spreading the message of Maa Parashakti, or supporting its activities, every contribution adds to the mission of spiritual and societal upliftment.

Together, let us work towards a future where Maa Parashakti’s teachings illuminate lives and inspire generations to come.
