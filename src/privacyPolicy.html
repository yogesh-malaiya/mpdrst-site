Donor Privacy Policy

MAA PARASHAKTI DHARM RAHASYA SEWA TRUST and its website https://dharamrahasya.com take your privacy seriously and treat all financial information about any transaction as highly confidential.

The Foundation deeply values all contributions to sustain our mission. To protect the privacy of our donors and their special relationship with the Trust, we maintain the following policies:

Our Policies

  • We may request personal information such as name, address, phone number, email, and credit card number ONLY for accepting donations.
  • We will not release or use this information for other purposes without your consent.
  • We do not trade or sell your personal information.
  • We offer donors the option to be recognized anonymously.
  • Donors may request not to receive solicitations or specific mailings.
  • We protect personal information with secured logins and regular passcode changes.

Donor Rights

  • To be informed of the Trust’s mission and how donations are used.
  • To access the Trust’s most recent financial statements.
  • To receive appropriate acknowledgment and recognition.
  • To expect confidentiality of donation information.
  • To ask questions and receive prompt answers when making a donation.

What Information We Collect

We collect donor information for processing payments and communicating about our activities. This includes:

  • Name, amount donated, address, phone, email, and comments.
  • Data visible on checks for donations made via check.

How We Use Your Information

Donor data is used for:

  • Distributing receipts and thanking donors.
  • Informing donors about upcoming activities.
  • Record keeping and internal analysis.
  • Compliance with government reporting requirements.

Security

We are committed to protecting donor information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure through robust security practices.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Donor Privacy Policy, contact us at:

Email: info@dharamrahasya.com
